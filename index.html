01 // THE MACRO

In-Situ Resource Utilization

NASA relies on biological computers for deep space missions. Transporting abiotic plastics to Mars imposes an insurmountable mass penalty. The solution is biological ISRU: utilizing lithoautotrophic bacteria to synthesize bio-polymers offline, in the harshest environments conceivable.

02 // THE LOCAL

The Terrestrial Translation

The infrastructural vacuum of a Martian colony perfectly mirrors the logistical realities of ultra-rural West Africa. Commercial grids in Ghana experience severe voltage sags under 207V for 22% of their operational time. If a biological system can survive Mars, it can survive the terrestrial grid.

03 // THE MICRO

Programming the Cell

We strip away the cloud and the hardware. By engineering synthetic plasmids into *E. coli*, we create orthogonal AND logic gates. The cell itself acts as the microprocessor, calculating environmental toxicity and thermal drift natively.

SYSTEM STRESS-TEST

The "Dumsor" Phenomenon

Designing a bio-foundry without accounting for electromechanical physics results in catastrophic failure. Commercial grids in Ghana experience severe under-voltage spells (< 207V) for roughly 22% of operational time. Scroll velocity dictates grid stability.

MOTOR TORQUE LOSS

A 15% voltage sag causes an exponential drop in induction motor torque, stalling bioreactor impellers and degrading the oxygen mass transfer coefficient.

THE HYPOXIC CASCADE

Without aeration, dissolved oxygen is consumed in < 30 seconds. ArcA~P represses terminal oxidases, forcing a massive upregulation of pflAB for mixed-acid fermentation.

PMF COLLAPSE (pH 4.0)

Toxic acetate accumulation overwhelms the media buffer. The influx of protons dissipates the transmembrane ΔpH gradient, collapsing the Proton Motive Force (PMF) required for ATP synthesis.

RE-OXYGENATION CRISIS

Grid restoration blasts pure air into a hypoxic liquid. The resulting burst of Reactive Oxygen Species (ROS) fractures genomic DNA via the Fenton reaction. The recovery is more lethal than the blackout.
